Learn about my dog
Belle

My dog has been there for me through all of my trials and successes
since she became a part of our family in 2017. She is your typical crunchy white dog
and she has stolen my heart. I'm literally dead she has my heart with her.

This is Belle and she is my heart and soul trapped in that little body with a big ribcage and oddly long legs. Me and my family first met her when we went to a rescue dog event. She was there with her two brothers, and they had been taken away from a hoarder's home, so she was able to leave emotionally unscarred(at least I think). Belle enjoys tanning, laying on the windowsill and in her bed, playing with tiny toys that could most definitely choke her, and eating things that she should not be eating.

My plan with her is first to turn her into an international superstar. Here is my inspo.

She will be trained in runway walks and skills, which will be a little hard since she is terrible at taking orders. She is very my-pace and won't be willing to listen to me whatsoever if I don't have a treat in hand, even if she has known the tricks for years. So the plan is to put posters like this one to keep her motivation up and her motor running.
